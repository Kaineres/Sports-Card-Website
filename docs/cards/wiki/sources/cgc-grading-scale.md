# CGC Grading Scale

CGC's published grading standards page covering all 20 grade levels from Poor 1 to Pristine 10.

## TL;DR

- 20-grade scale (1 → Pristine 10) with half-grades at every step; all grades fully defined in text. `[Certain]`
- Two "10" labels: Pristine 10 (50/50 centering, all criteria perfect) and Gem Mint 10 (55/45 front / 75/25 back, one criterion below Pristine). `[Certain]`
- CGC explicitly distinguishes TCG card criteria from sports/non-sports criteria at several grade levels — unique among major graders' published standards. `[Certain]`
- Centering published per grade from 9 down (not 8.5 for sports/non-sports — gap noted). `[Certain]`
- CGC previously labeled what is now "Gem Mint 10" as "Gem Mint 9.5"; those slabs now display as "Gem Mint 10." `[Certain — from verify-card-certification raw file]`

## Key claims

- Grades: Pristine 10, Gem Mint 10, Mint+ 9.5, Mint 9, NM/Mint+ 8.5, NM/Mint 8, Near Mint+ 7.5, Near Mint 7, Ex/NM+ 6.5, Ex/NM 6, Excellent+ 5.5, Excellent 5, VG/Ex+ 4.5, VG/Ex 4, Very Good+ 3.5, Very Good 3, Good+ 2.5, Good 2, Fair 1.5, Poor 1. `[Certain]`
- Pristine 10: 50/50 centering; special Pristine 10 label issued. `[Certain]`
- Gem Mint 10: 55/45 front / 75/25 back; one criterion does not meet Pristine requirements. `[Certain]`
- Mint 9: 60/40 front / 90/10 back for sports/non-sports; four sharp corners, minor wear, minor surface specs allowed. `[Certain]`
- 8.5 vs 8: Language is nearly identical; 8 explicitly adds 65/35 centering for sports/non-sports and "small-to-moderate" TCG handling defects vs 8.5's "small." CGC 8.5 centering for sports/non-sports not stated. `[Certain — gap noted]`
- NM/Mint 8: 65/35 for sports/non-sports. `[Certain]`
- Near Mint+ 7.5 and Near Mint 7: both 65/35 front for sports/non-sports. `[Certain]`
- Ex/NM 6: 75/25 for sports/non-sports. `[Certain]`
- VG/Ex+ 4.5: 85/15 for sports/non-sports; extremely noticeable diamond cut allowed. `[Certain]`
- Very Good+ 3.5: 90/10 centering. `[Certain]`
- Diamond cut terminology used at multiple grade levels. `[Certain]`
- Fair 1.5: "One catastrophic flaw" (staple hole, missing surface area, severe creasing, writing); may be miscut. `[Certain]`
- Poor 1: Multiple catastrophic flaws; severe creasing breaking card surface; missing cardstock portions. `[Certain]`
