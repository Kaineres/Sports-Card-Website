# BGS Grading Overview (beckett.com/grading)

## TL;DR

- BGS's public-facing grading landing page. Covers the 3-label system (Black/Gold/Silver), four-subgrade philosophy, service types, 4-tier service structure, Pristine 10 Black centering tolerance, and new label security features.
- Fills the "BGS service tiers not yet ingested" gap from [[grading-service-tiers]].
- Confirms Silver as a third label (all grades below Pristine/Gem Mint), which was not captured in [[bgs-grading-scale]].

## Key claims

- **3-label system** `[Certain]`: Black (Perfect Pristine 10 — all four subgrades = 10), Gold (Pristine 10 & Gem Mint 9.5), Silver (all other grades).
- **Service tiers** `[Certain]`: Base (75+ business days), Standard (45 business days), Express (15 business days), Priority (5 business days). All prices per card; specific dollar amounts not captured (pricing table loaded behind dynamic UI).
- **Extras** `[Certain]`: Autograph Card (+$5/card), Oversized Card (+$8/card), Relabel (from $9.95/card), Graded Card Review (+$0/card), Recase BGS only (from $9.95/card).
- **Pristine 10 Black centering** `[Certain]`: 50/50 front, 55/45 or better back.
- **Label security features** `[Certain]`: holographic metallic material, hidden UV pattern, anti-counterfeit light reactive badge, QR code linking to cert verification at beckett.com.
- **Service types** `[Certain]`: Card Grading, Grading + Autograph, Authentic Only.
- **Card scope** `[Certain]`: sports, gaming (Pokémon), and non-sports (Star Wars, etc.).
- **Half-points confirmed** `[Certain]`: "grades in half-point increments (i.e., 10, 9.5, 9, 8.5 etc.)"; half-points "share characteristics from both the level above and the level below."
- **Four subgrades on the label** `[Certain]`: "the only grading company to display all four condition scores — centering, corners, edges, and surface — directly on the label."
- **Authenticity check included** `[Certain]`: every card examined for trimming, doctoring, and alteration before grading begins.
- **Turnaround times are estimated** `[Certain]`: stated explicitly; subject to change.
