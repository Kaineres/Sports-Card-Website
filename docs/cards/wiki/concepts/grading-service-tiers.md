# Grading service tiers

## TL;DR

Grading services structure their pricing as a menu of tiers. Two axes drive cost: **declared card value** (which sets the insured-value cap during handling) and **desired turnaround speed**. Higher-value or faster-turnaround tiers cost dramatically more per card and may include additional features `[Certain]` — [[psa-grading-service]].

## The general shape

From PSA's offering `[Certain]` — [[psa-grading-service]]:

- **Lower tiers** — cheaper per card, slower turnaround, lower insured cap. Sometimes gated (PSA's "Value Bulk" is Collectors Club members only, 50-card minimum per era).
- **Higher tiers** — dramatically more expensive per card, faster turnaround, higher insured cap, plus additional features: high-resolution Premium Imaging, fitted sleeve protector inside the slab, Grader Notes documenting defects.

PSA's pricing curve is steep — endpoints span ~$25/card to ~$10,000+/card, a >400× range.

## Why declared value drives price

The insured-value cap rises with the tier `[Certain]` — [[psa-grading-service]]. The grading service is implicitly underwriting the card's value during handling, so higher-value cards must use higher-priced tiers. A $5,000 card cannot legally use a $500-cap tier.

## Why turnaround drives price

Turnaround speed is a separately-priced premium independent of value. PSA's tiers span from 140–160 business days at the bottom to 5–7 business days at the top `[Certain]` — [[psa-grading-service]]. Example: "Walk-Through" ($599, $10k cap, 5–7 BD) and "Premium 1" ($999, $25k cap, 5–7 BD) share turnaround — the insured-value cap is what differentiates them.

## Endpoints of PSA's tier range (as of 2026-05-18)

> ⚠️ Specific prices and turnarounds change frequently. For the live full list of all 14 tiers, see the raw source: `cards/raw/Official Trading Card Grading Service.md`, or the original page at `psacard.com/services/grading`. Endpoint snapshot:

- **Cheapest, slowest** — "Value Bulk": $24.99/card, $500 insured cap, 140–160 business day turnaround. Gated to Collectors Club members; 50-card minimum per era.
- **Most expensive, fastest** — "Premium 10": $9,999+/card, $250,001+ insured cap, 5–7 business day turnaround. Includes Premium Imaging + sleeve protector + Grader Notes.
- **Cheapest 5–7 day tier** — "Walk-Through": $599/card, $10,000 insured cap.

Total tier count: **14** `[Certain]` — [[psa-grading-service]].
