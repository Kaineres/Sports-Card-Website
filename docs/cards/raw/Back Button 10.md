## BECKETT GRADING SCALE

Every card is evaluated across four key subgrades and given an overall grade that reflects its unique condition. The chart below outlines the criteria behind each numeric grade.

- ### 2: Good
- ##### Centering
	90/10 both ways or better on front. 100/0 or offcut on back. Noticeable diamond cutting is allowed.
- ##### Corners
	Noticeably rounded or heavily notched corners with moderate layering.
- ##### Edges
	Severely chipped, notched or layered.
- ##### Surface
	Severe print spots. Noticeable creases. Noticeable color or focus imperfections. Noticeable border discoloration. Heavy wax stains. Moderate ink mark or tape stain. A surface devoid of gloss. Noticeable scuffing or a noticeable tear.

##### Half-Point Grades

Please note that Beckett Grading Services provides final grades in half-point increments (i.e., 10, 9.5, 9, 8.5 etc.). Cards that are assigned a grade with a half-point increment typically share characteristics from both the level above and the level below the actual grade given.
