## BECKETT GRADING SCALE

Every card is evaluated across four key subgrades and given an overall grade that reflects its unique condition. The chart below outlines the criteria behind each numeric grade.

- ### 9.5: Gem Mint
- ##### Centering
	55/45 both ways on front. 60/40 or better on back.
- ##### Corners
	Virtually flawless to the naked eye with only slight imperfections allowed under magnification.
- ##### Edges
	Virtually flawless to the naked eye with only specs of wear visible under magnification.
- ##### Surface
	Virtually flawless to the naked eye with a few extremely minor print imperfections visible under magnification. Deep color, devoid of registration or focus imperfections. Devoid of scratches and metallic print lines.

##### Half-Point Grades

Please note that Beckett Grading Services provides final grades in half-point increments (i.e., 10, 9.5, 9, 8.5 etc.). Cards that are assigned a grade with a half-point increment typically share characteristics from both the level above and the level below the actual grade given.
