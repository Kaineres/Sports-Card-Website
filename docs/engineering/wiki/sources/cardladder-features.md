# Card Ladder — Features & Pricing (Source Summary)

## TL;DR
- Card Ladder is a sports card analytics and price guide with 100M+ sales back to 2000, a proprietary player index, and integrated pop reports from all 4 grading services.
- Pro tier: $20/mo or $200/yr (raised from $15/$150 in Feb 2025). Free tier has meaningful functionality.
- No decision synthesis — lookup and data presentation only.

## Key Claims

- "The industry's only total market index for thousands of players and characters." `[Likely — self-claim]`
- Tracks every public sale dating back to 2000 — longer historical depth than named competitors. `[Likely]`
- Data sources: eBay, Goldin, Heritage Auctions, Fanatics Collect, and others. `[Certain]`
- Pop report integration from PSA, BGS, SGC, and CGC in card detail views. `[Certain]`
- Card Ladder Value: price modeling using player index + individual card's historical relationship to that index. Fills gaps for cards with no recent sale. `[Certain]`
- Pro pricing: $20/month or $200/year as of February 1, 2025 (was $15/$150). `[Certain]`
- Free tier includes: browse, daily news, sales recaps, Card Ladder Index, marketplace links. `[Certain]`
- Pro adds: advanced filters, sorting algorithms, saved searches, full sales history, in-depth analytics. `[Certain]`
