# CollX Pro — Features & Pricing (Source Summary)

## TL;DR
- CollX is a free card scanning and collection app with a peer-to-peer marketplace. Pro tier unlocks unlimited collections, AI assistant, CSV export, and marketplace perks.
- Pro: $9.99/mo or $99.99/yr. Free tier limits collection to ~500 items.
- Hobbyist and community framing — not positioned toward investor analytics. No decision intelligence.

## Key Claims

- Free tier: card scanning, pricing, ~500-item collection, peer-to-peer marketplace, social feed. `[Certain]`
- Pro adds: 500 AI-enhanced Scan+ credits/month, unlimited collections, $10/mo marketplace credit, CSV export, CollX AI (hobby Q&A), pin cards, bulk pricing, group chats, featured listings, priority support, iOS family sharing. `[Certain]`
- Pro pricing: $9.99/month or $99.99/year. `[Certain]`
- Card Dealer Pro (desktop multi-marketplace listing software): 3-month free trial included with Pro. `[Certain]`
- No price alerts, no market indexes, no decision synthesis. `[Certain]`
- Free tier anchors user WTP at $0 — noted as a monetization weakness vs. investor-tool competitors. `[Certain — reasoning]`
