# Alt Platform — Features & Pricing (Source Summary)

## TL;DR
- Alt is a vertically integrated sports card platform: portfolio tracking + authenticated-only marketplace + physical Delaware vault. Raised $106M. Targets premium ($1k+) graded cards.
- High transaction fees (9–15% seller, 15% buyer premium at auction). No fractional ownership.
- No decision intelligence. Underserves the $50–$500 modern-graded beachhead that is SlabMetrics' target.

## Key Claims

- Only graded, authenticated cards listed on exchange. `[Certain]`
- Alt Portfolio: daily card valuations from recent comps and algorithm; single-screen collection view. `[Certain]`
- Alt Exchange: fixed-price listings and timed auctions. Searchable by sport, player, year, grade, brand. `[Certain]`
- Alt Vault: physical storage in Delaware, insured, with high-res camera verification. `[Certain]`
- Alt Vault storage: free for cards listed for sale; ~$20/card/year for private storage. `[Likely — fee structure may have changed]`
- Seller fees: 9% marketplace, 7% auction (vaulted), 12.5% auction (non-vaulted). `[Likely — verify at alt.xyz]`
- Buyer fees: 15% buyer premium + 3% payment processing. `[Likely — verify at alt.xyz]`
- Raised $31M Series A + $75M Series B. Notable investors: Naval Ravikant, Tom Brady, Giannis Antetokounmpo, Kevin Durant, Alexis Ohanian, John & Patrick Collison. `[Certain]`
- Alt Lending (card-backed loans): planned, not launched as of source date. `[Likely]`
- No buy/hold/sell recommendation feature. `[Certain]`
