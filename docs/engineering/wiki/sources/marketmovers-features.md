# Market Movers — Features & Pricing (Source Summary)

## TL;DR
- Market Movers is a multi-tier sports card platform with comp data, collection tracking, price alerts, deals, and Intelligence Reports (grade/player/variation ratio analysis).
- Three paid tiers: $9.99 / $24.99 / $49.99/month. 75,000+ users. Founded 2020 by Geoff Wilson and Tyler Nethercott.
- Intelligence Reports (Unlimited only) is the closest incumbent feature to analytical signal generation — surfaces market inefficiencies from grade and variation ratios.

## Key Claims

- Data sources: eBay, Fanatics Collect, Goldin, Heritage Auctions, Pristine Auctions, Cards HQ, MySlabs, PSA, SGC, Beckett, CGC. Updated daily. `[Certain]`
- Tens of millions of sales comps back to 2001. `[Certain]`
- Starter ($9.99/mo): comps, charts, trends, deals, 5 price alerts, 25-item collection. `[Certain]`
- Premium ($24.99/mo): 250 items, 20 alerts, Market Pulse (macro hobby index). `[Certain]`
- Unlimited ($49.99/mo): unlimited items/alerts, Intelligence Reports, private founder calls, partner discounts. `[Certain]`
- Annual plans save 17%. `[Certain]`
- Intelligence Reports: grade ratios, player ratios, variation ratios — described as finding market inefficiencies "before the hobby catches on." `[Certain]`
- Community layer: monthly livestreams, giveaways, private Q&A calls (Unlimited tier). `[Certain]`
- No buy/hold/sell recommendation synthesis. `[Certain]`
