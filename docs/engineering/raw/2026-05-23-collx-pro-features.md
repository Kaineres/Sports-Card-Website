# CollX Pro — Features & Pricing

## Overview

CollX is a free sports and TCG card scanning app with an optional Pro subscription. Primary hook is photo-based card identification and instant pricing. Has a built-in social layer and peer-to-peer marketplace ("Deals"). Large user base from free entry.

## Free Tier

- Card scanning to identify and price cards
- Collection tracking (up to ~500 items)
- Peer-to-peer marketplace (buy/sell/trade with other users)
- Community feed and social features (follow other collectors, make offers)

## Competitive Position
- Hobbyist-default framing. Entry via free scan-to-price, not investor analytics.
- Marketplace-first: peer-to-peer trades and sales are the core engagement loop.
- No decision intelligence, no signal synthesis, no buy/hold/sell recommendations.
- Free tier suppresses willingness to pay for analytics features — users anchored at $0.
- Mobile-first; large user base but not the investor-identity segment.
